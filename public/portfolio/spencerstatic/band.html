Band

Spencer McGillicutty is a co-ed indie pop group based out of Minneapolis, MN.

Featuring a rotating cast of four different lead singers, the band delivers wistful bedroom jangle pop, dreamy girl group send-ups, and ambling, tuneful folk with equal aplomb.

Their third album All The Happy People! is due out May 15, 2012.

Spencer McGillicutty is (from left to right):

  • Nicole Mellas - Vocals
  • Ryan Ruff Smith - Vocals, Guitar, Harmonica
  • Brittany Miller - Vocals
  • Mitchell Johnson - Vocals, Acoustic and Electric Guitars, Piano, Pretty much everything under the sun....

The Spencer McGillicutty Players are:

Jeff Engholm, Tyler Tholl, Peter Johnson, Ted Godbout, Jessica Scherer, Bill Blatzheim, Jed Anderson, Mike Rubbelke, Nick Syman, Teddy Kain, Danny Vitali, and many more...
